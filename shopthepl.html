Using technology to highlight independent people, products, services, talent, and so much more. Shop the PL offers an option for sellers and service providers to get connected with leads and customers that want to support independent.

  • 01 We Find Independent

    Shop the PL brings people, products, and services together across various categories and industries.

  • 02 Shop & Schedule

    Find Products & Services all in one place. Find your new favorite brands and services.

  • 03 Safe Checkout
